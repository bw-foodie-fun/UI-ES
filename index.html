FOOD MEETS FUN

Foodie Fun is like scrapbooking, but instead of just looking, you get to eat what's inside the photo! It is a fun way to keep a track of meals you have eaten already, and restaurants you have visited. If you are eating out a lot or very forgetful, you will love us!
